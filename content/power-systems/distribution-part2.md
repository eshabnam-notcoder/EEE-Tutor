# Radial Distribution System

A radial distribution system is a type of electrical distribution system where power flows in one direction from the source to the consumer.

<img src="assets/animations/radial-distribution-system.svg" alt="Radial Distribution System">

---

## Working Principle

- Power flows from substation to consumers in a single path  
- Each consumer is fed from one feeder only  

---

## Advantages

- Simple design  
- Low initial cost  
- Easy to operate  

---

## Disadvantages

- Poor reliability (fault affects entire section)  
- Voltage drop increases at far end  
- Not suitable for large cities  

---

## Simple Understanding

Imagine a tree branch: if the main branch breaks, everything beyond it loses power.

# Transmission Systems - Part 3

Transmission lines are classified based on their length and electrical behavior.

<img src="assets/animations/transmission-part3.svg" alt="Transmission Line Types Animation">

---

## Types of Transmission Lines

### Short Transmission Line

- Length < 80 km
- Capacitance ignored

---

### Medium Transmission Line

- 80 km to 250 km
- Capacitance considered

---

### Long Transmission Line

- > 250 km
- Distributed parameters considered

---

## Important Concepts

### Ferranti Effect

Voltage at receiving end becomes higher than sending end in long lines.

---

### Corona Effect

Ionization of air around conductors causing power loss and noise.

---

## Key Points

- Line length determines model used
- High voltage lines reduce losses
- Corona increases with high voltage

---

## Simple Understanding

Different transmission lines behave differently depending on how long they are.

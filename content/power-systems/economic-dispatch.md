# Economic Dispatch

Economic Dispatch is the process of determining the optimal allocation of power generation among available power plants to meet the load demand at minimum cost.

<img src="assets/animations/economic-dispatch.svg" alt="Economic Dispatch Animation">

---

## Objective

- Minimize total generation cost
- Meet load demand
- Maintain system constraints

---

## Basic Condition

`Incremental Cost of all generators should be equal`

`dC1/dP1 = dC2/dP2 = ... = λ`

Where:
- C = Cost function
- P = Power output
- λ = Lambda (incremental cost)

---

## Constraints

- Power balance:  
  `ΣP = PD + losses`

- Generator limits:
  `Pmin ≤ P ≤ Pmax`

---

## Key Points

- Used in power system operation
- Reduces fuel cost
- Improves efficiency
- Important for grid optimization

---

## Simple Understanding

Economic dispatch decides how much power each power plant should produce so that electricity is generated at the lowest possible cost.

# Distribution System Layout

An electrical distribution system is the final stage of the power system where electricity is delivered from substations to consumers.

<img src="assets/animations/distribution-system-layout.svg" alt="Distribution System Layout">

---

## Main Components

- Distribution Substation  
- Feeders  
- Distributors  
- Service Mains  
- Consumers  

---

## Basic Layout

Power flows in the following order:

Generating Station → Transmission System → Distribution Substation → Feeders → Distributors → Consumers

---

## Key Points

- Final stage of power delivery  
- Operates at lower voltage levels  
- Designed for safe and reliable supply  
- Closest part of system to consumers  

---

## Simple Understanding

Think of it like a water supply system where water comes from a main tank (substation) and is distributed through pipes (feeders and distributors) to homes (consumers).

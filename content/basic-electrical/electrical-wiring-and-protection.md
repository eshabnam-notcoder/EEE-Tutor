# Electrical Wiring and Protection

Electrical wiring distributes electrical power safely, while protection devices prevent damage caused by faults such as overloads and short circuits.

<img src="assets/animations/electrical-wiring.svg" alt="Electrical Wiring and Protection Animation">

---

## Common Protection Devices

### Fuse

Protects circuits by melting when excessive current flows.

---

### MCB (Miniature Circuit Breaker)

Automatically disconnects power during overload or short circuit.

---

### RCCB

Protects against electric shock due to leakage current.

---

### Earthing

Provides a safe path for fault current to the ground.

---

## Key Points

- Prevents electrical accidents.
- Protects appliances and wiring.
- Earthing reduces shock hazards.
- Proper wiring improves safety and reliability.

---

## Applications

- Residential wiring
- Commercial buildings
- Industries
- Electrical panels

---

## Simple Understanding

Protection devices act like safety guards that disconnect electricity whenever something goes wrong.

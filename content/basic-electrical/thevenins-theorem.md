# Thevenin’s Theorem

Thevenin’s Theorem states that any linear two-terminal network can be replaced by a single voltage source in series with an equivalent resistance.

<img src="assets/animations/thevenin.svg" alt="Thevenin's Theorem Animation">

---

## Formula

`Equivalent Circuit = Vth + Rth`

Where:

- `Vth` = Thevenin Voltage
- `Rth` = Thevenin Resistance

---

## Key Points

- Simplifies complex circuits
- Useful for load analysis
- Applicable only to linear circuits
- Makes repeated calculations easier

---

## Simple Understanding

Replace a complicated circuit with one battery and one resistor that behaves exactly the same from the load's point of view.

# Measuring Instruments

Measuring instruments are devices used to measure electrical quantities such as voltage, current, resistance, power, and energy.

<img src="assets/animations/measuring-instruments.svg" alt="Measuring Instruments Animation">

---

## Common Instruments

### Ammeter

Measures electric current.

**Unit:** Ampere (A)

---

### Voltmeter

Measures voltage.

**Unit:** Volt (V)

---

### Ohmmeter

Measures resistance.

**Unit:** Ohm (Ω)

---

### Wattmeter

Measures electrical power.

**Unit:** Watt (W)

---

### Energy Meter

Measures electrical energy consumption.

**Unit:** kWh

---

### Multimeter

Measures voltage, current, and resistance using a single device.

---

## Key Points

- Essential for electrical testing.
- Improves troubleshooting.
- Used in laboratories and industries.
- Digital instruments provide higher accuracy.

---

## Simple Understanding

Electrical measuring instruments help us know what is happening inside a circuit by measuring different electrical quantities.

# Circuit Elements

Circuit elements are the basic components used to build electrical circuits. Every electrical circuit is made using one or more of these elements.

<img src="assets/animations/circuit-elements.svg" alt="Circuit Elements Animation">

---

## Types of Circuit Elements

### Resistor

A resistor limits or opposes the flow of electric current.

**Unit:** Ohm (Ω)

---

### Capacitor

A capacitor stores electrical energy in the form of an electric field.

**Unit:** Farad (F)

---

### Inductor

An inductor stores electrical energy in the form of a magnetic field.

**Unit:** Henry (H)

---

### Voltage Source

A voltage source provides electrical voltage to a circuit.

Examples:

- Battery
- Generator
- DC Power Supply

---

### Current Source

A current source supplies a constant current regardless of the load.

---

## Key Points

- Resistors oppose current.
- Capacitors store electric charge.
- Inductors store magnetic energy.
- Sources supply electrical energy.
- Circuit elements are either active or passive.

---

## Simple Understanding

Every electrical circuit is built using basic building blocks like resistors, capacitors, inductors, and power sources. Each component performs a specific function in controlling electricity.

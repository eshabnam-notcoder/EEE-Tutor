# Transformers

A transformer is a static electrical device that transfers electrical energy from one circuit to another through electromagnetic induction.

<img src="assets/animations/transformer.svg" alt="Transformer Animation">

---

## Types

### Step-Up Transformer

Increases voltage and decreases current.

---

### Step-Down Transformer

Decreases voltage and increases current.

---

## Basic Formula

`Vp / Vs = Np / Ns`

Where:

- V = Voltage
- N = Number of turns

---

## Main Parts

- Primary Winding
- Secondary Winding
- Magnetic Core

---

## Key Points

- Operates only with AC supply.
- Uses electromagnetic induction.
- No moving parts.
- Highly efficient electrical device.

---

## Applications

- Power transmission
- Distribution systems
- Chargers
- Power supplies

---

## Simple Understanding

A transformer changes voltage levels without changing the frequency of electricity.

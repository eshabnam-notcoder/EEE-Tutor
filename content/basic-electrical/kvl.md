# Kirchhoff’s Voltage Law (KVL)

Kirchhoff’s Voltage Law (KVL) states that the algebraic sum of all voltages around any closed loop is equal to zero.

<img src="assets/animations/kvl.svg" alt="Kirchhoff's Voltage Law Animation">

---

## Formula

`∑V = 0`

---

## Key Points

- Based on the conservation of energy
- Applied to closed electrical loops
- Used for mesh (loop) analysis
- Valid for both DC and AC circuits

---

## Simple Understanding

As you travel around a closed circuit, the total voltage gained is equal to the total voltage lost.

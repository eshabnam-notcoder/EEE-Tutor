# Electrical Machines

Electrical machines are devices that convert electrical energy into mechanical energy or mechanical energy into electrical energy.

<img src="assets/animations/electrical-machines.svg" alt="Electrical Machines Animation">

---

## Types

### DC Generator

Converts mechanical energy into DC electrical energy.

---

### DC Motor

Converts DC electrical energy into mechanical energy.

---

### Induction Motor

The most widely used AC motor in industries.

---

### Synchronous Machine

Operates at synchronous speed and is used as a motor or generator.

---

## Key Points

- Motors convert electrical energy into motion.
- Generators produce electrical energy.
- Transformers are also considered electrical machines.
- Essential in industries and power plants.

---

## Applications

- Electric vehicles
- Pumps
- Fans
- Elevators
- Power generation

---

## Simple Understanding

Electrical machines either produce electricity or use electricity to perform mechanical work.

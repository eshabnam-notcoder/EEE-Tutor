# Alternators and Transformers

Power system components are essential devices used in generation, transmission, and distribution of electrical energy.

<img src="assets/animations/alternator-transformer.svg" alt="Alternator and Transformer">

---

## Alternator

An alternator converts mechanical energy into electrical energy (AC).

### Key Points:
- Used in power plants  
- Works on electromagnetic induction  
- Produces alternating current  

---

## Transformer

A transformer transfers electrical energy between circuits at different voltage levels.

### Key Points:
- Works on mutual induction  
- Used to step-up or step-down voltage  
- No frequency change  

---

## Simple Understanding

- Alternator = produces electricity  
- Transformer = adjusts voltage for transmission or usage

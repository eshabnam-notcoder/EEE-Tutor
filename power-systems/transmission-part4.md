## ABCD Parameters

Used to represent transmission line behavior.

### Equations

`V_s = A V_r + B I_r`

`I_s = C V_r + D I_r`

---

## Important Effects

### Skin Effect

Current flows mainly on the surface of conductor at high frequencies.

---

### Proximity Effect

Current distribution changes due to nearby conductors.

---

## Sag and Tension

- Sag: vertical dip in conductor
- Tension: pulling force in conductor

---

## Key Points

- ABCD parameters simplify analysis
- Mechanical design affects line safety
- High voltage lines require careful design

---

## Simple Understanding

Transmission lines can be studied using equations and physical effects that affect performance and safety.

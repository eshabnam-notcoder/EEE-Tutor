# Ring Main Distribution System

A ring main system is a distribution system where the feeder forms a closed loop, supplying power from both directions.

<img src="assets/animations/ring-main-system.svg" alt="Ring Main Distribution System">

---

## Working Principle

- Substation feeds power in a closed loop  
- Each consumer can be supplied from two directions  
- Fault can be isolated without interrupting supply  

---

## Advantages

- High reliability  
- Better voltage stability  
- Fault isolation is easy  

---

## Disadvantages

- Higher cost than radial system  
- More complex protection system  

---

## Simple Understanding

Think of a circular road: if one path is blocked, you can still reach your destination from the other side.

# Transmission Systems - Part 1

Power transmission is the process of transferring electrical energy from power plants to distribution systems using high-voltage lines.

<img src="assets/animations/transmission-part1.svg" alt="Transmission System Animation">

---

## Need for Transmission

- Power plants are located far from load centers
- Reduce power losses
- Enable bulk power transfer
- Interconnect power systems

---

## Structure of Transmission System

- Step-up transformer
- Transmission lines
- Substations
- Step-down transformer

---

## Types of Transmission Lines

- Overhead transmission lines
- Underground cables
- HVDC transmission lines

---

## Key Points

- High voltage reduces current
- Low current reduces losses
- Transmission is part of power grid backbone

---

## Simple Understanding

Transmission lines act like highways that carry electricity from power plants to cities.
